Reasarch Introduction

My research interests mainly include label-efficient tasks in Machine Learning, Computer vision, and Time series analysis, such as unsupervised learning, semi-supervised learning, and self-supervised learning. Besides, I work on applying these methods to solving Geoscience problems.
